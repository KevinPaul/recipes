Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup oatmeal
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese
- 3 egg whites (2/3) cup
- 2 packets of stevia
- cinnamon to taste
- Walden Farms pancake syrup

Directions:
1. Grease skillet with pam (or coconut oil) and bring to medium heat.
2. Mix ALL ingredients together in bowl (or puree in food processor).
3. Cook on skillet for about 2 minutes, then flip and leave on heat until firm.
4. Top with Walden Farms Pancake Syrup or Cinnamon and Stevia
